# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, respectful, and collaborative community.

## Expected Behavior

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Be respectful and constructive in communication.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions.
- Accept feedback gracefully.
- Focus on technical merit and project goals.
- Help others learn and contribute effectively.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination in any form.
- Insults, personal attacks, trolling, or derogatory comments.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.
- Repeated disruptive behavior in discussions or reviews.
- Any conduct that is inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including:

- issue discussions,
- pull requests,
- code reviews,
- project communication channels.

It also applies when an individual is officially representing the project in public spaces.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing acceptable behavior. They may take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to behavior they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

Possible actions include:

- warning,
- temporary moderation,
- temporary ban,
- permanent ban from community interactions.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to project maintainers through repository issues or direct communication channels designated by maintainers.

When reporting, include:

- what happened,
- where it happened,
- links/screenshots/logs if available,
- any relevant context.

All reports will be reviewed and handled as promptly and confidentially as possible.
